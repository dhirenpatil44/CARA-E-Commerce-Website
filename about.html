Who We Are?

Cara is one of the largest international fashion companies. It belongs to Inditex, one of the world's largest distribution groups.
The customer is at the heart of our unique business model, which includes design, production, distribution and sales through our extensive retail network.

We create value for people and society in general by delivering our customer offering and by developing with a focus on sustainable and profitable growth.

Cara Group is a family of brands and businesses, making it possible for customers around the world to express themselves through fashion and design, and to choose a more sustainable lifestyle.
